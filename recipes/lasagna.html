Lasagna

This is the worlds most delicious lasagna

Lasagna is a psata dish that is made by staking layers of lasgna in between different fillings. There are many different types of fillings that can be used such as ground meats, tomato sauce, vegetables, and cheeses. This dish is often times topped with cheese which melts when baked.

This dish is very popular and orignated from Italy. This dish is often served for groups of people and is very popular for moms to make in the world. This is considered to be a not great tasting food in movies and tv shows

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pund ground pork
  • onion
  • canned tomatos
  • 2 tablespoons of parsley
  • 1 curshed clove of garlic
  • sugur
  • dried basil
  • dried oregano
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • sugur
  • uncooked lasagna noodles
  • cottage cheese
  • parmesan
  • shredded mozzarella
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Combine the 1/2 pound of ground beef and pork and then cook the meat ina skillet until browned and crumbly
  2. Add the onion and continue to cook until its translucent
  3. Stir in the canned tomatos, half of the parsely, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugur
  4. Boil the lasgna noodles in lightly salted water until they are al dente
  5. mix the cottage cheese,parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  6. layer the ingredients alternating between the meat and cheese and noodles
  7. cover in foil and bake at 375 F for 30 min
  8. remove the foil and continue to bake until the top is golden brown
